# Attribution — `human.glb`

`human.glb` is the **"Michelle"** character mesh distributed with the
[three.js](https://github.com/mrdoob/three.js) project under
`examples/models/gltf/Michelle.glb`.

- **Origin:** Adobe **Mixamo** (auto-rigged character), redistributed in the
  three.js examples.
- **three.js** itself is MIT-licensed, but the individual example *assets* carry
  their own terms.
- **Mixamo terms:** characters and animations from Mixamo are royalty-free to use
  in your own projects (games, apps, film). Adobe's terms restrict
  **redistributing the standalone character files** as if they were your own
  asset. Using it inside the Corpus app is fine; re-publishing `human.glb` as a
  downloadable asset on its own is the gray area.

## If you need a clean redistribution license

- **Procedural, license-free:** `python make_human_glb.py` → builds a human from
  scratch (no third-party asset at all).
- **CC-BY 4.0:** the `CesiumMan` model from
  [KhronosGroup/glTF-Sample-Assets](https://github.com/KhronosGroup/glTF-Sample-Assets)
  (attribute Cesium).
- **Ready Player Me:** generate your own avatar and use its permanent
  `https://models.readyplayer.me/<id>.glb` URL under their terms.

Swap the file and update this note accordingly.
