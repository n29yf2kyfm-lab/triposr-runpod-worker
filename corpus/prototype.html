How it works

From measurements to composition

What's real today runs the middle and right steps. The photo→3D scan (left) is the SAM 3D Body pipeline in phase0/ and needs a GPU.

01 / needs GPU

Photo → 3D mesh

A single photo becomes a 3D body mesh via SAM 3D Body on RunPod. Vertex landmarks give waist, hip, arm and leg measurements automatically.

02 / real now

Measurements → body fat

Eight NHANES-aligned measures feed an XGBoost regressor trained on real 2017-2018 DXA scans. This is live below.

03 / real now
